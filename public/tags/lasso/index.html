Beyond LASSO

The basics One of the first ‘machine learning’ many will come across is LASSO regression. This may suggest that it’s an elementary method; something to get you started on machine learning before moving to the more interesting topics. But there is a sea of methods that are adaptions of LASSO. The goal of this blog post is to give a brief overview of the landscape of these methods and where they might be useful.
